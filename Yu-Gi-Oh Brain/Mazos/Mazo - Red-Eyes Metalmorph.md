## Combos principales
1. **Metalmorph setup**: NS Red-Eyes Black Meteor Dragon → search Metalmorph → activate Metalmorph → equip to a RE monster → tribute for Fullmetal Dragon
2. **Dragoon line**: Activate Red-Eyes Fusion → send REBD + Dark Magician → summon Dragoon (if Dark Magician available)
3. **Fullmetal win**: Fullmetal equipped with Heavy Metalmorph → gains ATK from banished Metalmorphs → direct attack + burn
4. **Gearfried pop**: Gearfried + Metalmorph → pop opponent's card each turn

## Tech choices
- **Inferno Fire Blast**: Burn for game after Fullmetal + Metalmorph boost
- **Foolish Burial**: Send REBD to GY for Wyvern/Gearfried setup
- **Red-Eyes Baby Dragon**: SS from hand when REBD is destroyed, extension

## Matchups
- **Vs Snake-Eye**: Fullmetal Dragon es torre (no targeteable, no destroy), favorable
- **Vs Yubel**: Fullmetal no puede ser superado por Yubel, favorable
- **Vs Tenpai**: Desfavorable, mazos combo explosivos ganan antes que Fullmetal se active
- **Vs Labrynth**: Favorable si Metalmorph no es popped
